Anvesh is an open-source marketing intelligence tool designed to simplify data aggregation across multiple channels. Born out of the need for a unified dashboard that respects user privacy while providing deep insights, Anvesh leverages modern web technologies to deliver real-time analytics without the bloat of enterprise software.

Our mission is to democratize marketing data for indie developers and small teams. By providing a self-hostable solution, Anvesh ensures that your data remains yours, eliminating third-party tracking risks and reducing subscription fatigue.
